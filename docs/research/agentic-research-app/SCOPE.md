# Research Scope: Agentic Research App

**Date:** 2026-03-28
**Project:** Agentic Research App — CLI-based AI research agent
**Brief:** [PROJECT-BRIEF.md](../PROJECT-BRIEF.md)

## Research Questions

1. What is the competitive landscape for AI-powered research agents?
2. What are the technical feasibility details for building with Python + Claude API + ChromaDB?
3. Which niche (real estate, stocks, AI dev) has the best profitability potential?
4. What are the known gaps and user complaints with existing tools?
5. What search API, embedding model, and content extraction approach should we use?

## Research Areas

- **Competitive Landscape** — Major players, open-source alternatives, niche tools
- **Technical Feasibility** — Claude tool use, search APIs, web scraping, RAG, streaming, evals
- **Niche Profitability** — Market size, data availability, willingness to pay, competition intensity
- **User Pain Points** — What existing tools get wrong, where opportunities exist
