# Research Scope

## Topic: AI Applications for Ski Ticket Ecommerce
## Purpose: Identify and rank the highest-impact AI use cases for a ski resort operator's online ticket shop, with technical feasibility assessment for a NopCommerce headless POC.
## Date: 2026-03-29

## Key Questions
1. What competitors and alternatives exist? How do they use AI?
2. Which AI use cases deliver the most ROI for resort operators?
3. What technical approaches and architectures make sense?
4. What data would a resort have available to power these features?
5. How are AI agents discovering and booking travel inventory?
6. What does group ski trip booking friction look like today?
7. Can the MVP features be built with synthetic/mock data?

## Research Areas
- Competitive landscape (Inntopia, Liftopia, resort-direct, adjacent verticals)
- Technical feasibility (NopCommerce headless, AI service integration)
- Domain knowledge (ski ecommerce funnel, buyer behavior, revenue models)
- Emerging patterns (agentic AI booking, group coordination, dynamic pricing)
