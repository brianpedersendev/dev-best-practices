# Research Scope: AI Woodworking Sidekick

## Topic
AI-powered woodworking project planning tool for hobbyist woodworkers

## Purpose
Validate the AI Woodworking Sidekick concept and inform the implementation plan. Determine if there's a viable market, assess technical feasibility, map the competitive landscape, and recommend go/no-go.

## Key Questions
1. What competitors and alternatives exist? How do they compare?
2. What technical approaches are others using in this space?
3. What are the known pitfalls and failure modes?
4. Is the core value proposition actually differentiated?
5. What would the technical architecture look like?
6. Is Gemini the right model, or should we consider alternatives?
7. System prompts vs. RAG for woodworking domain knowledge?
8. How do we ensure AI-generated dimensions and cut lists are accurate?
9. What monetization model fits this market?
10. Where do woodworkers hang out online and how do we reach them?

## Research Areas
- Competitive landscape (direct AI tools, adjacent plan/design tools, general LLM usage)
- Technical feasibility (LLM accuracy for structured plans, knowledge architecture, validation)
- Market signals (hobbyist woodworking market size, spending, demand signals)
- Domain knowledge (joinery rules, material dimensions, tool assumptions)
- Monetization models (SaaS, marketplace, freemium patterns in maker space)
- Distribution strategy (SEO, community, content marketing)
